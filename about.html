Features

  • Stat Comparison: Compare different builds side-by-side to see which one suits your playstyle best.
  • Save & Share (coming soon): Save your builds for future reference and share them with friends or the wider community.

How to use

First up you have to enter your stat names at the middle to declare and keep track of which stats are you comparing. Name both of your builds at the top of each build card. Then enter the stat values for each build in the respective input fields. As you enter the values, the comparison indicators in the middle will update in real-time to show which build has higher stats for each category.
